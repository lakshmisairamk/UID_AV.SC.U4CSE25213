Amrita Vishwavidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (or Amrita University) is a multi-campus private deemed university in India
It currently has 19 constituent schools spread across ten campuses in Coimbatore, Amritapuri (Kollam), Kochi, Bengaluru, Amaravati, Chennai, Faridabad, Mysore, Nagercoil and Haridwar
Accredited with the highest possible A++ grade by NAAC and ranked as 7th best university in India in National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2024Coimbatore campusIt is headquartered in Ettimadai, Coimbatore
The other ten campuses are satellite off-campuses of the same university as per section 3 of the University Grants Commission Act, 1956
It offers over 300 undergraduate, postgraduate, integrated-degree, dual-degree, doctoral programs in engineering, medicine, management, architecture & planning, natural sciences, Ayurveda & health sciences, agriculture & life sciences, commerce, Arts & humanities, social sciences, media & communication, law, fine arts and cultural studies
