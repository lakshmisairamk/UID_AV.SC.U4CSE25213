About the Campus

The Amritapuri campus is the spiritual heart and headquarters of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, situated in the peaceful coastal town of Kollam, Kerala. Founded by the beloved humanitarian Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma), this campus blends spiritual values with academic excellence. Overlooking the serene Arabian Sea and tranquil backwaters, Amritapuri nurtures both intellect and character, offering a truly holistic education experience in a uniquely beautiful natural setting.
